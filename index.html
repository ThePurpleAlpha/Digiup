Quick Guide

What Makes a CV Actually Work

Most CVs fail before a human even reads them. Here's what separates the ones that get through from the ones that don't.

🎯
Use Keywords from the Job Posting

ATS systems filter by keywords. Mirror the language in the job description—don't paraphrase.

📐
Keep It One or Two Pages Max

Recruiters spend 6–10 seconds on a first pass. Make every line count. Cut the fluff.

🚫
Avoid Tables, Columns & Graphics

Most ATS systems can't read them. Stick to a clean, single-column layout for maximum compatibility.

📊
Quantify Everything You Can

"Grew sales by 40%" beats "responsible for sales growth" every single time.
